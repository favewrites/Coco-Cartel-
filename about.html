Our Story

Born from a love of beauty and a belief that luxury should feel personal — this is Coco Cartel.

Our Heritage

Crafted for the
Extraordinary

Every Coco Cartel piece begins with a single vision: to create jewelry that transcends time. Our artisans combine centuries-old techniques with contemporary design, using only ethically sourced precious metals and gemstones.

From the initial sketch to the final polish, each creation undergoes over 40 hours of meticulous handcrafting. This is not jewelry. This is wearable art.

We started Coco Cartel with one conviction — that a woman who wears our pieces should feel it. Not just on her wrist or around her neck, but somewhere deeper. A quiet confidence. A sense of arrival.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Ethical Sourcing

Every gemstone and metal is sourced responsibly. We work only with suppliers who share our commitment to fair trade and environmental integrity.

Timeless Design

We don't follow trends. We set them — and then we outlast them. Our designs are conceived to be worn for decades, not seasons.

Made with Love

Each piece is made to order by master artisans who treat every creation as their own. No mass production. No shortcuts. Only craft.

Global Reach

From Lagos to London, Paris to Seoul — Coco Cartel ships worldwide. Luxury should have no borders.

The Process

Where Science
Meets Soul

We believe luxury should feel personal. That's why every piece is made to order, tailored to the unique story of its wearer. Our design studio collaborates with master jewelers across three continents.

The result? Collections that feel both timeless and urgently modern — jewelry that doesn't just accessorize, but transforms.

Our process begins with a hand-drawn sketch. From there, a wax mould is cast, refined, and set by hand before the final piece is polished to perfection. Every stage is done by a human hand, never a machine.
